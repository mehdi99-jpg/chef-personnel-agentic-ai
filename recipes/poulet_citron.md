# Recipe: Lemon and Thyme Chicken / Poulet au Citron et Thym (Local Secret)
Keywords: chicken, poulet, meat, citron, lemon

Ingredients:
- 2 chicken breasts / blancs de poulet
- 1 lemon (juice and zest) / citron
- 2 fresh thyme sprigs / thym
- Olive oil, salt, pepper

Instructions:
1. Marinate the chicken with lemon and thyme for 30 min.
2. Pan-sear 6-8 minutes per side.
3. Serve hot. (Warning: This recipe contains lemon).
